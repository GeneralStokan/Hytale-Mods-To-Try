A curated list of amazing Hytale mods worth checking out!

View on GitHub

Here are some mods that I believe are worth checking out! These mods are either alternatives to popular mods that haven't updated in a while or those I or you believe are better alternatives. Check out the discussions to talk about these alternatives!

I will also be adding newly released mods that deserve a spotlight. There will also be popular mods in the list that are still getting regular updates and support, but ones that seem abandoned or have a better alternative may not be included.

Submit a Pull Request to add your own mods you believe should be on the list or check out discussions to recommend mods to be added!

Quality of Life

Mod Description Links
Floating Workbenches
by: PhilXD		 Workbenches no longer break when the floor beneath is broken CurseForge
Better Chest
by: JoshMC		 Chests no longer break when the floor beneath is broken CurseForge
Item Magnet
by: Jaredlll08		 Adds a craftable magnet to pull in items CurseForge
Better Magnetism
by: Linceros		 If you don't want to have a craftable magnet and instead just an increased pickup range this is the best mod I've found for that. Good customization CurseForge

Utilities

Mod Description Links
Blocchio
by: Yriokiri		 A WAILA mod that has active developers, a clean UI and great customization CurseForge
Better Item Viewer
by: Drexhd		 An in-depth JEI-type mod that has good customization with a very clean and easy to navigate UI. Ability to pin recipes, give yourself items, sort by mod and item types. Has advanced item info like weapon damage and tool durability CurseForge
HyUi
by: EllieAU		 "simplify the creation and management of custom User Interfaces for Hytale" - EllieAU CurseForge
Auto-Multi Hud
by: Darimus		 Similar to "MultipleHud by: Buuz135" but it doesn't require other Mods to "add support" in order for it to work CurseForge

Gameplay Enhancements

Mod Description Links
Farming+
by: Kaiwoknats		 Adds new fertilizers and composting. Also adds tiered hoes, sickles, and watering cans each with their own area of effect. A couple nice QOL features too CurseForge
Farming Overhaul
by: Krynnx		 Adds a new hoe, watering can spreads farther, seeds now drop when harvesting. Doesn't add as much as "Farming+" above but they both have their benefits and I really enjoy this mod CurseForge
Hidden's Harvest Delights
by: HiddenIsme		 Add 34 new foods and new fruit trees. Foods have detailed models and they have different effects/rarities CurseForge
MiniMap-Waila
by: DaddyDaDuck		 Alternative to "Wayfinder by: Alexr03" which crashes my game and others have claimed corrupted their world. Adds a customizable minimap, decent performance and also adds a toggleable WAILA option CurseForge

Adventure & Content

Mod Description Links
RPG Companion
by: Stefman1234		 Adds a companion to the game! Equip gear, level them up and go on adventures. I will say this mod is pretty new and there are still bugs to figure out. Mod developer has short- and long-term goals CurseForge
RPG Leveling and Stats
by: Zuxaw_Dev		 A popular mod that gets regular updates and features. Completely overhauls the progression system, adds player level and stats as well as mob levels CurseForge
MMO Skill Tree

Endgame QOL
by: Lewaii		 This is another case that the most downloaded mod of its kind is the best I've found of its kind. Adds a TON of QOL and content to the game, mostly endgame stuff hence the name CurseForge
